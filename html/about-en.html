Our Story

At the peak of the global Covid-19 crisis, amidst the confusion and fear, a powerful truth emerged. Individual actions, no matter how small, can create a formidable collective strength. “Stay at Home, Save Lives” became more than just a slogan; it became a testament to our shared resilience and unity. From this crucible of global challenge, YESAction (Youth for Environmentally Sustainable Action) arose, embodying the spirit of awareness, advocacy, and action.

Our Commitment

At YESAction, we are unwavering in our promise to build a more sustainable future through intention, integrity, and action. Each chapter, partnership, and initiative reflects our deep-rooted dedication to environmental responsibility.

This is more than a mission — it's a promise to our communities, our planet, and future generations. With collective resolve, we strive to create lasting, meaningful impact.

2020–2021

This period was characterized by in-depth research, campaign conversations, and the establishment of our base structures. Forming our inaugural initiative, we began an awareness drive encouraging sustainable living practices. By 2021, YESAction had its first 10 chapters nationwide.

2021–2022

During the foundational year, as the world grappled with the pandemic, a profound realization took root. Choosing the collective efforts in China to combat the crisis, the birth of YESAction was marked in September 2021, and our first chapter was officially established.

2021–2022

Our efforts gained significant traction, resulting in the expansion of our chapter network to over 18. With an aim to address diverse global issues, initiatives like “YESClimate,” “YESOcean,” and “YESWaste” were launched and implemented.

2023–Present

Our journey took an upward trajectory, with our network stretching to over 70 chapters globally. New initiatives including YESCooking, which promotes sustainable kitchens via culinary practices, were introduced. With a continuous wave of innovation, we remain committed to creating and launching even more impactful initiatives.
