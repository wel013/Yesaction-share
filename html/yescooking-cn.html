Who is YESCooking?

YESCooking is a proud initiative of YESAction. Our focus? To champion the many benefits of cooking at home. While grabbing a fast food meal might seem like the easiest choice, trust us, whipping up a home-cooked dish can be just as effortless. Plus, it comes with added benefits like health and sustainability.

The Magic of Home-Cooked Meals

When you opt for fast food, you inadvertently contribute to the colossal waste produced annually, not to mention most choices are laden with grease. In contrast, cooking at home gives you the power of choice. Decide your portions, pick fresh ingredients, and tailor your meals to your liking. Not only does home-cooking consume less energy and packaging, but it also fosters lifelong habits. Embrace cooking early on and discover treasured recipes and experiences that will stay with you.

Beyond just a task, cooking can be a communal activity, a self-improvement tool, and a deep-seated passion. So, why not embark on this flavorful journey of transformation with us?

Stay Connected with YESCooking

For those eager to dive deeper, our blog is a treasure trove. From handy tips to dodge fast food cravings, to insightful reads on its health and environmental repercussions, and a collection of simple yet delectable recipes, we’ve got you covered.

And for our visual enthusiasts, our Instagram is bursting with infographics on the fast food vs. home-cooked debate. Plus, stay tuned for engaging video tutorials on easy-to-make dishes!
