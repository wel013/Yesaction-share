YESACTION (Youths for Environmentally Sustainable Action) is a national youth-based 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated towards empowering youths to be the change in their community for environmental sustainability.

Awareness

We raise awareness among youth about environmental sustainability and Net Zero goals.

Action

YESACTION was founded as a call for all people, governments, universities, and students to take bold action.

Advocacy

We advocate for policies and solutions to achieve environmental justice and systemic change.

Vision Statements

Environmental sustainability is an undeniable global imperative that surpasses boundaries, politics, cultures, and time. We are determined to be the preeminent student platform, fervently seeking groundbreaking solutions from corporations, organizations, and governmental bodies. From carrying out White House Initiatives on Climate Change to the unwavering climate commitments of the United Nations for Carbon Zero, we want to proudly lead the charge towards a sustainable future. Together, we possess boundless potential to bring about transformative change.

Initiatives

The main objective of being a YESAction chapter is to make a meaningful impact at the local level. Chapters are expected to show leadership in implementing YESAction initiatives in their schools and communities that promote sustainability. These initiatives can include activities such as learning about sustainability, educating others, conserving energy, testing water quality, fundraising, outreach, and more.
